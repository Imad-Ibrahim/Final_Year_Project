ABOUT

Share Wheels provides an opportunity for people to travel together to the same event. Created with the environment in mind, Share Wheels connects fans going to the same events together, and offers them an opportunity to carpool. If you canâ€™t drive? No problem! You can still sign up to be a passenger to all events!


The primary advantage of carpooling to events rather than driving individually is that there is reduced carbon emissions. If one car is able to bring 5 people, there is 80% less carbon emissions going into the atmosphere than if each person was to drive individually. There is also 80% less fuel consumed.
On a broader scale, carpooling reduces the traffic on our road network. On an individual level, it is financially cheaper to carpool to an event. The money saved on fuel can be used to purchase something at the event itself!


It is our belief that by sharing wheels to and from events we can have a positive impact on the level of carbon emissions we produce on our way to our favourite events. By sharing wheels, we also get the opportunity to meet and travel with fellow fans!
It is for this reason that, Share Wheels is a win-win opportunity for both the environment and event goers!


Note: Drivers who have their rate between 0 and 1.9 stars, they will NOT match with any passengers.
